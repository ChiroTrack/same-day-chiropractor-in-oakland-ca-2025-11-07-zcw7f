About Oakland Accident Care

Founded in 2015, Oakland Accident Care emerged from a simple mission: to provide compassionate, accessible chiropractic care to car accident victims in our community. Our founder, Dr. Michael Rodriguez, recognized a gap in the market for clinics that understood the urgency of post-accident injuries and the financial burden they place on families. Starting with a small team of two chiropractors in a modest clinic, we've grown to become one of Oakland's most trusted names in accident injury care, treating thousands of patients and earning their trust through consistent, quality results.

Today, we remain committed to our core values of compassion, expertise, and accessibility. We believe that every patient deserves prompt, professional care regardless of their financial situation. Our team of licensed chiropractors continuously updates their skills through advanced training, and our Spanish-speaking staff ensures that language is never a barrier to excellent care. We're not just treating injuries—we're helping our neighbors rebuild their lives after traumatic accidents.
